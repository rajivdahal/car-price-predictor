For the past few years we have been observing the sites that provide second-hand cars in Nepal. We noticed that none of the sites provided a reasonable price even for the used cars. They were charging too much given qualities provided for the car. So we came up with this idea to help predict the correct and reasonable price for the second-hand cars in Nepal

This is basically an idea to use the concept of linear regression for predicting the price of second-hand cars in Nepal.

We use the key factors that affect the price of cars such as car model,engine size,type of fuel used by cars,kilometers run,lot number,etc. These factors are linearly dependent on the price of car, so to determine the best fit line of the relation between these factors and price of car, their linear relationship plays an important role. Considering these facts we can conclude that the process of determining price using linear regression will provide the best and reasonable price of cars in Nepal.



The process involves firstly the collection of data about used cars from dealgara.com and identifying important features that reflect the price of cars. Then it involves preprocess and removal of NAN values, and discarding of the features that are not relevant for price prediction.Afterwards linear regression is applied on the preprocessed dataset with features as inputs and price as the output. Finally the predicted price of the desired cars is shown as the output to the users. The accuracy and Root Mean Square Error(RMSE) of the model is calculated using sklearn.
